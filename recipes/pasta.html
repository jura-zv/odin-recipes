Pasta

Simple and cheap

We all know that buying groceries is pretty expensive these days. That is why I present you the ultimate dish for saving extra money.

Ingredients

  • spaghetti
  • salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 all-beef hot dogs
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • garlic oil

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of slightly salted water to a boil point over high heat.
  2. Add 0.5kg of dry spaghetti to the boiling water and boil for about 10 minutes.
  3. Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a large frying pan over medium heat until shimmering. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant, 30 seconds to 1 minute. Then add hotdog and cook for about 4 minutes.
  4. Mix together finished spaghetti with the hotdogs and add 1/2 cup ketchup
  5. Voila! Your food is ready to eat.
